Why We Started TotalScope

After running a successful roofing & restoration company for a number of years, we noticed that our in-house estimates far out-paced those of our fellow contractors. First one, then another, then more and more were asking us to write their estimates for them, as they were getting larger and larger claims easily approved. We turned this into our side hustle, then one day found that roofing had become the side hustle after writing estimates and supplements was taking most of our time. The writing was on the wall, so we decided to go all-in on TotalScope. We created a data-collection app that has taken the industry by surprise, and by storm. Our proprietary process in creating top-notch estimates is allowing us to serve you, our clients, better and better every day by increasing not only your bottom line but also decreasing your time spent on each project. So, now you can go fishing, or do another project, the choice is yours. Either way, we got you covered.
